

Elevator System

- Floors in the building: 1-10
- No. of elevators: 1
- At each floor: Indicator for direction of travel
- Panel: To stop the elevator at each floor.
- When the elevator reaches a floor : OpenTime and CloseTime




- ElevatorObject
  - currentFloor
  - requestFloor
- ElevatorDoor object
- Indicator Panel
	- Indicator -> How the elevator is travelling
	- Switch Panel
		- Floor 1: only button to go up
		- Floor 10: Only button to do down
		- Floors 2 - Floor 9: Two buttons to go up and down
- Floor Object
